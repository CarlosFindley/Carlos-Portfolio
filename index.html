About Me

I have a passion for user friendly and functional websites. I started my career in corporate technology compliance (SOC, FedRAMP, ISO, PCI DSS, and more). This gave me the opportunity to work with numerous engineering teams. As a technology aficionado, I have enjoyed solving technical problems through team collaboration. This has translated to expanding my education in software development. I’m currently attending UC Berkeley Extension’s Full-Stack software development bootcamp. Growing up, I lacked access to computers. Having a career in technology was just something I didn’t think about; it was out of the question. Fortunately, I’ve been blessed to have supportive friends and a community that has pushed me forward. As such, I believe in democratizing technology. I believe that technology can be a tool to improve people’s lives. As a life-long learner, I love spending time exploring different subjects. Lately, I’ve been learning about cryptography and Latin American history. I also love hiking the East Bay trails with my dog Lancelot.
